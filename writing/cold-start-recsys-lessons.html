Lessons from Cold-Start Recommendation in Production

A concise set of patterns for cold-start systems at scale.

  • Optimize the first-audience funnel: candidate quality and exploration policy matter more than late-stage tweaks.
  • Pair uplift-aware exploration with uncertainty-aware ranking to stabilize quality on sparse signals.
  • Measure impact with both ecosystem growth metrics and user-quality guardrails.
